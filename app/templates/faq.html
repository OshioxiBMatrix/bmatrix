Quick Questions

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for all new customers. You can access all features during this period with no credit card required.

Can I change my plan later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel your subscription anytime. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. Your service will continue until the end of your current billing period.

Can other info be added to an invoice?

Yes, you can add custom information to your invoices such as PO numbers, company details, or special notes through your account settings.

How does billing work?

We bill on a monthly or annual basis, depending on your chosen plan. Payments are processed securely through our payment providers.

How do I change my account email?

You can update your email address in your account settings. After changing, you'll need to verify your new email address.
